Alsancak

Actually, I don't have much to say about Alsancak. It is a place in the west of Kyrenia, mostly hosting holiday hotels. It is about 10 minutes by car to Kyrenia. at distance. Since our hotel is in Alsancak, I wanted to give a brief information about the sea. Rather than the center of Kyrenia as a beach, its east and west are recommended because it has a long sandy beach.
