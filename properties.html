Mediterra

Every expectation and need of our clients will be met quickly and efficiently in our drive to maintain total customer satisfaction.In all we do,we will work to proudly represent our country in international partnership and relationships.

3 Bedroom Villa

STYLISH AND LIMITED

Mediterra’s villas are located at the most beautiful point of the land. Right next to the valley, they have their own private gardens and optional swimming pools.

Along with the breathtaking panoramas, the layouts were designed to perfectly suit the modern lifestyle. Open floor plans are perfect for entertaining and family life, with the perfect amount of privacy. The living/dining space, peaceful verandas, and beautiful garden provide ample opportunity for both.

Villas are the favorites of the homeowners who would like to enjoy outdoor space with their family or the community or just prefer more privacy.
