Our History

From past to present...

Pioneering the North Cyprus real estate sector with its signature projects for 37 years, Kıbrıs Developments has adopted the principle of reflecting all kinds of details that will appeal to today's needs, facilitate, and beautify life, based on historical, cultural, traditional and environmental awareness.

The projects realized by Kıbrıs Developments offer a lifestyle that is far beyond a residence, specially designed with every detail, creating added value not only for the residence itself but also for life.
